Mission Brief

A former Department of Planetary Security employee leaked a folder called help.zip and claimed it came straight from Rocky. He could not make sense of it. You get the three surviving files, a trail of hidden metadata, and one job: figure out where Rocky was taken before the trail goes cold.

The evidence jumps from an author's old web history to binary hidden in an image, then into a sealed file that only opens if you notice the right astrophage-sized clue. Follow the crumbs, recover the coordinates, and identify the mountain before whoever moved Rocky disappears again.

There is no special answer wrapper for this challenge. URLs, handles, and extensions are checked forgivingly, so focus on the important part of the answer instead of exact formatting.
