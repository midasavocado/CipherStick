Food Jam

2–5 Players Ages 6+ 15–25 Minutes

Game Overview

In Food Jam, you race to collect the right ingredients, trade smart, and play spicy action cards to complete recipes for points. Two-ingredient recipes score 2, three-ingredient recipes score 3. Simple… until someone drops an Oops, Spill or hits you with a Mix-Up.

🎲 Roll to see your moves.
🛒 Draw ingredients or recipes.
🔁 Play actions & trade.
🍽️ Cook recipes to score.

Rules (Quick View)

Objective: Most recipe points wins 2-ingredient = 2 pts 3-ingredient = 3 pts Hand limit: 2 recipes & 5 ingredients
Setup
  1. Shuffle each deck separately: Recipe, Actions, Ingredients.
  2. Place decks face down; make discard piles for Actions & Ingredients.
  3. Deal each player 3 ingredients and 1 recipe.
Gameplay
  1. Youngest starts.
  2. Roll: 1–2 = 1, 3–4 = 2, 5–6 = 3 moves.
  3. Moves: draw, discard, trade, or play an action.
Winning
  • When the last recipe is drawn, put the Golden Spoon on it (+1).
  • When that recipe is cooked, the game ends. Most points wins.
