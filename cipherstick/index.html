Browser-Based OSINT Challenges for Digital Detectives

What is OSINT?

OSINT—short for Open Source Intelligence—is the art of gathering powerful information from public sources. Think detective work without a badge. It's used by journalists to verify facts, by cybersecurity experts to track threats, and by curious minds to uncover hidden stories in plain sight.

With OSINT, your browser becomes your lab. You inspect websites, decode data, analyze metadata, and follow digital breadcrumbs—all with tools anyone can access. No hacking. No scripts. Just skill.

How It Works

Every challenge is a single page that runs entirely in your browser. No login, no server-side processing. Open the file, explore the clues, and practice the same investigative habits used by journalists, analysts, and cybersecurity teams worldwide.

Because nothing leaves your machine, these puzzles are perfect for classroom demos, conference workshops, or private skill-building.

Why Certificates Matter

When you crack the final answer, a PDF certificate is generated instantly, stamped with your name, the date, and the puzzle title. This quick proof of completion is ideal for professional development logs, job interviews, or bragging rights on social media.

Certificates render locally with JavaScript libraries, so your data stays put—no central database, no privacy worries.
