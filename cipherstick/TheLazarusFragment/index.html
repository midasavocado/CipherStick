Obituary: Dr. Aldous Grey

Winter 2025. DARPA’s neural resilience program Lazarus was shelved after rumors it could fracture a mind into digital shards. Dr. Aldous Grey was declared deceased under “unusual circumstances.”

Dr. Aldous Grey, beloved researcher, is remembered
for a life devoted to the study of memory, resilience,
and the fragments we leave behind. He is survived
by his questions, which refuse to rest. He once wrote:
“Every ghost begins in the margins.”
