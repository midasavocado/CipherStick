Getting Started

Flux uses advanced natural language processing to convert your descriptions into executable iOS Shortcuts. To begin, navigate to the Create interface.

Example Prompt:

"Create a shortcut that takes a screenshot, crops it to the center, and saves it to my 'Work' album."

FAQ

Is Flux free?

Yes, the Starter plan allows for up to 5 generations per day at no cost.

How do I install the shortcut?

After generation, click the "Download" button. This will provide a signed .shortcut file. Open this file on your iOS device or Mac to add it to your library.

Privacy & Security

Flux generates shortcuts on-the-fly. We do not store the content of your shortcuts after generation is complete.
